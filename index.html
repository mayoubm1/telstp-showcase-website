About TELsTP

Pioneering Egypt's Life Sciences Renaissance

The Tawasol-Egypt Life Science Technology Park (TELsTP) is a visionary mega-project designed to transform Egypt into a global epicenter of life sciences research and innovation. With an investment of 8 Billion Egyptian Pounds and a 5-year development timeline, TELsTP is designed as a fully integrated, eco-conscious ecosystem encompassing education, research, healthcare, and sustainable community development.

Spanning 200 acres, this comprehensive ecosystem aligns with Egypt's Vision 2030 and is poised to create 12,000 to 25,000 new jobs while contributing significantly to Egypt's GDP and knowledge economy.

Message from the Chairman

Dear Visionaries and Pioneers of Scientific Excellence,

As we stand at the threshold of a new era in scientific discovery, TAWASOL Life Science Park represents more than just a research facility—it embodies humanity's boldest aspirations for understanding the very essence of life and consciousness.

Throughout history, Egypt has been a cradle of knowledge and innovation. From the ancient wisdom of our ancestors who built the pyramids and pioneered early medicine to the modern scientists who continue to push boundaries, our nation has always sought to illuminate the path of human progress.

Today, we honor that legacy by creating a space where the brightest minds can converge, where cutting-edge technology meets compassionate purpose, and where the mysteries of life sciences can be explored with both scientific rigor and ethical mindfulness.

The TAWASOL Life Science Park is founded on a simple yet profound principle: that scientific advancement must serve humanity. Our mission transcends mere academic pursuit or commercial gain—we seek to transform knowledge into solutions that improve lives and create lasting value for generations to come.

In this extraordinary collaborative environment, we will nurture the next generation of scientists, physicians, and innovators. We will forge partnerships across borders and disciplines. We will develop technologies and treatments that address the most pressing health challenges of our time. And we will do so while honoring our responsibility as stewards of both scientific progress and human dignity.

I invite you to join us on this remarkable journey—whether as a researcher, student, investor, or supporter. Together, we will write a new chapter in Egypt's illustrious history of contribution to human knowledge and wellbeing.

With unwavering commitment to excellence and service,

Dr. Ahmed Hassan Al-Mansouri
Chairman, TAWASOL Holding
